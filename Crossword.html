W hy Play Crossword?

The game is intellectually challenging. 

It gives you some jumbled letters, and asks you to guess a corresponding word made up with them. You may enter your guessing, and click "Confirm" to see if you are correct.

There are three levels with increasing difficulty. To pass each level, you need to guess 6 words correctly. In the first level, you will at least get 6 points; in the second, 12; in the third, 18. 

Does this mean each time you pass the final level, you get a total points of 36? Definitely not!

The faster you guess a word, the more points you get from that click. It means, your ultimate points depend on how fast you react. Each time you see your ultimate points increase, you are making progress!

If you are not confident with your reaction speed, don't worry! The game will give you clues.
