CROSSWORD

GAME

DEVELOPER: Qianhe Liu

Welcome to Crossword! Please guess a word

from the jumbled letters.

There are three levels of increasing difficulties.

You will get 1 point for guessing one correctly in

the first level, 2 points for guessing one correctly

in the second level, 3 points for guessing one

correctly in the third level.

The players who take less time gets extra points,

so let's see who get the most in the end!
